# Primetrade_Analysis

Analysis Summary:
This analysis explored the relationship between Bitcoin market sentiment (Fear & Greed Index) and trader performance on Hyperliquid DEX using 52,491 trades across 2024. The findings reveal a strong positive correlation between bullish sentiment and trader profitability — Extreme Greed days generated $2.32M in realized PnL with a 47.5% win rate, while Fear days resulted in -$86K PnL with only a 25.8% win rate. Over 93% of total trading volume occurred during Greed and Extreme Greed periods, confirming that traders are significantly more active in bullish conditions. Analysis of trade direction shows that most traders are sentiment-followers rather than contrarians — long bias increases as sentiment rises, while short positions during Fear periods still result in net losses. Extreme Fear proved to be the most dangerous environment with only an 8.2% win rate, suggesting risk management breaks down during panic conditions. Based on these insights, a sentiment-aware trading strategy is recommended — increasing position sizing during Greed phases, reducing exposure during Fear, and avoiding high-leverage trades during Extreme Fear periods.
